Finally the wait is Over....It's time for ELEGANTS to SPREAD OUR WINGS, ALL OVER THE WORLD with a National Level Techinical Symposium TRANCE-2K24 (Trends & Recent Advances in Communication and Electronics) This is a blend of both the technical & non-technical events. TRANCE is a fest organized by the Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) branch. The fest consists of six sub-events, each represented by a letter in the word TRANCE, and covers a wide range of technical and non-technical topics relevant to the ECE field.

What is T R A N C E actually ?

TRANCE is a fest consists of six sub-events, each represented by a letter in the word TRANCE

  • 01 T Stands for

    "TECHNOGEEK" is an exciting coding event designed for students who are passionate about technology and coding. This event provides an opportunity for students to showcase their coding skills and compete with other students. The event is open to all students, regardless of their level of experience, and offers a chance to win prizes and certificates.

  • 02 R Stands for

    "REJUVENATE" event, attendees can expect to participate in a variety of activities, including public speaking workshops and Technical version of LUDO. Additionally, there may be keynote speakers and expert panel discussions on topics such as leadership, motivation, and personal growth.

  • 03 A Stands for

    "ARTGALLERY" is an exciting event that celebrates creativity and talent in the arts. This event is designed for students who are passionate about art and offers an opportunity to showcase their skills in front of a wider audience. The competition is open to all students, regardless of their level of experience, and offers a chance to win prizes and certificates.

  • 04 N Stands for

    "Nexus" is an exciting event that brings together students and engineering enthusiasts from various domains. The event is designed to provide a platform for individuals to explore their engineering minds and engage in innovative discussions, collaborations, and workshops.At the "Nexus" event, attendees can expect to participate in a variety of activities, including panel discussions, keynote speeches, technical workshops, and interactive exhibits. The event will also feature networking opportunities with experts and peers from various engineering fields.

  • 05 C Stands for

    "Conquer" is a dynamic platform designed to help individuals sharpen their minds and improve their cognitive abilities. The platform is focused on providing tools and resources to help individuals develop their critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.At "Conquer," attendees can expect to participate in a range of activities and workshops that are designed to challenge and stimulate the mind. These might include logic puzzles, brain teasers, and strategic games that help to develop analytical and creative thinking skills. Additionally, there may be keynote speakers and expert panels discussing the latest research and strategies for enhancing cognitive function.

  • 06 E Stands for

    "Exposure", the ultimate event for showcasing your creative ideas in the form of PPTs or posters. At Exposure, we provide a platform for individuals from diverse fields, such as business, education, research, and arts, to showcase their innovative ideas in a visually appealing manner.Our event offers a unique opportunity for individuals to present their ideas in an engaging and interactive way, using PPTs or posters as a scaffold. With our state-of-the-art technology, we ensure that your ideas are presented in a professional and dynamic manner, leaving a lasting impression on the audience.

Highlights

* The event is a National Level Technical Symposium called TRANCE-2K24.

* The theme of the event is "Trends & Recent Advances in Communication and Electronics".

* The event is organized by the Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) branch.

* TRANCE-2K24 consists of six sub-events, each represented by a letter in the word TRANCE.

* The event includes both technical and non-technical events.

* The event aims to showcase recent advances in the ECE field and provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills and knowledge.
